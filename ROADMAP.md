# Roadmap

## V1 (Current)

Dispatch transforms meeting notes and business requests into structured, governed engineering workflows.

It classifies requests, gathers context, produces solution briefs, enforces approval gates, and prepares execution while maintaining a complete audit trail.

## V2 (Work in Progress)

Dispatch will evolve into an AI Engineering Orchestrator.

After human approval, Dispatch will be able to delegate implementation to autonomous coding agents (such as Codex, Devin, Cursor, or other compatible agents) by generating structured engineering packages instead of raw prompts.

Rather than writing production code itself, Dispatch will coordinate the engineering lifecycle by:

* generating implementation-ready specifications
* creating repositories, branches, and engineering tasks
* dispatching approved work to coding agents
* tracking execution progress
* collecting pull requests and validation results
* maintaining governance, approvals, and auditability throughout the delivery process

The long-term vision is to become the orchestration layer between business intent and autonomous software engineering.
